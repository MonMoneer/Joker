Key Design Decisions

  • 5 rows × 3 columns — header, top player, middle (left | trick | right), info+bids, my cards
  • Opponent card backs are TINY (22×30px mobile, 28×40px desktop) — they just indicate how many cards, not readable
  • Bid buttons sit IN the info row — same line as "bid: X · won: X", no overlay needed
  • Player's cards get the full bottom row — never cut off, never overlapped
  • Left/Right players are narrow columns (56px mobile, 80px desktop) — avatar + small vertical card stack
  • Trick zone fills the remaining center space — grows/shrinks with viewport
  • Trump card shown in trick zone corner — small, rotated, low opacity
  • 100dvh height — accounts for mobile browser chrome
